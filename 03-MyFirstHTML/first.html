How To Make A Simple Sandwich

Ingredients Needed

  • Sliced sandwich bread
  • Favorite sliced meat (cold cut)
  • Favorite condiments
  • Favorite Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles

Directions

  1. SGather the materials needed on a flat ,clean, and sanitary surface. Take out two slices of your favorite bread. Some of the most common types of bread are white, wheat, sourdough, multigrain, etcâ€¦
  2. Open your favorite condiment jar. Insert spreading knife into the jar and load the desired amount of condiment onto knife. Then spread condiment evenly onto one side of a sliced piece of bread. You can repeat this step for any additional condiments that you wish to eat. You can mix some of the condiments together to try a variety of different flavors. Which condiments you put on your sandwich is only limited by your imagination.
  3. Choose your favorite meat(s) and place onto one of the pieces of bread on the side with the condiment. Make sure to spread the meat in a way will go all the way to the edge of the bread. If your meat is round, you can slice the meat in half and place the straight edges along different sides of the piece of bread. This will allow you to enjoy the desired meat flavor with every bite of your sandwich.
  4. Place your desired amount of the cheese you have chosen on top of the meat. Make sure add enough cheese to cover the entire sandwich.
  5. Place the desired toppings (sliced tomatoes, pickles, lettuce, etc.) on top of your cheese.
  6. Place the second slice of bread with the condiment covered side down on top of your toppings and enjoy.
